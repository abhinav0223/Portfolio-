About Me

I'm a passionate and curious Computer Science student with a deep interest in emerging technologies like Data Science, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. I enjoy working with data, discovering patterns, and turning numbers into meaningful insights that can drive real-world impact.

My journey in tech started with web development—building responsive, interactive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React. Over time, I’ve expanded into backend development with Node.js and Python, while also diving into data analysis using tools like Jupyter Notebook, Pandas, and TensorFlow.

What truly excites me is the intersection of code and intelligence—whether it’s training a model to predict outcomes, automating a task using Python, or visualizing complex data. I’m always exploring new projects that challenge me to think analytically and creatively.

Outside of coding, I enjoy tech podcasts, working on open-source contributions, and participating in hackathons. I believe in lifelong learning and love connecting with like-minded individuals who are equally excited about technology, innovation, and problem-solving.
